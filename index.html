Welcome to INation!

INation is an app for citizens to participate in local politics. Users can vote on polls about issues submitted by others in their area. The app also allows users to report issues directly to their elected officials, including pictures and location details. Officials can then prioritize these issues and citizens can track their resolution. By participating in iNation, users can have a say in shaping their communities.
